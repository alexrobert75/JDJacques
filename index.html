Biography

I am PhD student at LPSM Sorbonne Université under the direction of Lorenzo Zambotti.

My research focuses on renormalization theory of SPDEs and regularity structures.

Research interests

  • Stochastic analysis
  • Combinatorics
  • Differential geometry
  • Numerical methods
